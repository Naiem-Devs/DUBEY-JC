About Me

Hi! Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me. My name is Sach Dubey and I am a recent graduate from the University of California Davis where I studied Design and minored in Computer Science. I believe I chose this focus of study because of influences in my formative years, my mom is a practicing artist and my dad is a software engineer. The overlap of these two disciplines in the context of User Interface and Experience was the perfect fit for me because it allowed me to extend both boundaries of my curiosity. In my free-time I love to get creative in the realm of digital art, and find a lot of enjoyment in expressing myself through poetry.  On an average day you can probably find me in the Davis Student Community Center solving design challenges and conquering coursework. I also spend a lot of time leading the Davis Computer Science Club as President. In the past, I have worked on numerous user interfaces alongside a plethora of companies and organizations. I am excited for you to see my work!
